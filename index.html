09 Soulful Marketing

Simple. Soulful. Consistent.

Not everywhere. Consistently visible in 1–2 places. The 3-piece system: Magnet · Home · Invitation.

✦ Magnet

A free value piece that attracts ideal clients: mini reading, free meditation, downloadable ritual, short masterclass.

✦ Home

A simple place where people land: landing page, Skool community, "start here" link-in-bio.

✦ Invitation

Weekly or bi-weekly invitations to work with you. Posts, stories, emails that explicitly mention your offer and how to book. Invitations, not convincing.

10 Launch

Gentle 4-Week Launch

Soft but structured. Start with a beta cohort. Focus on conversations, not just posts.

Week 1 · Clarify & Prepare

+

Lock in the essentials before you tell a soul.

  • Finalize offer name, promise, structure
  • Lock in pricing (launch price)
  • Set up booking link (Calendly, Stripe, etc.)
  • Write boundaries & onboarding doc

Week 2 · Warm Up

+

Seed the problem & desire your offer addresses. No selling yet.

  • Share 3 story posts about the problem you solve
  • Email your list with a personal story
  • DM 5 warm leads with a check-in (no pitch)

Week 3 · Invitations

+

Now you invite. Clearly. Warmly. With receipts.

  • Public announcement post + email
  • 3–5 behind-the-scenes stories
  • Personal DMs to warm leads (with consent)
  • Share a mini client transformation or testimonial

Week 4 · Enrollment & Delivery

+

Onboard, over-deliver, gather feedback.

  • Send welcome emails / onboarding docs
  • Book kickoff calls
  • Request testimonials at midpoint + end
  • Journal what worked & what to refine
